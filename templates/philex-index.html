Meet Sade Black, Philex Records' breakthrough artist whose mesmerizing vocals and magnetic stage presence are redefining the boundaries of Afrobeats. With a voice that seamlessly blends soulful melodies with contemporary African rhythms, Sade Black represents the evolution of modern African music.

Her debut singles have already captured the attention of industry veterans and new audiences alike, showcasing her extraordinary ability to weave compelling narratives through her music. Sade Black's unique sound - a fusion of traditional Afrobeats with elements of R&B and soul - creates an irresistible sonic experience that resonates with listeners across generations.
